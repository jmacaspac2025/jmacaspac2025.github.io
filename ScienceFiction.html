Science Fiction

Dr. Stone

Dr. Stone is an anime dedicated to the wonders of Science. In 2019, a green light strikes Earth and turns every human into stone. Thousands of years later, Senku broke out of the stone and instantly started re-building civilization in the stone world. After de-petrifying his friends, they set out with a mission to free everyone from pertrification with the help of humanity's science. "This is exhilarating. Get excited!"

Vivy: Fluorite Eye's Song

Vivy is another anime revolving music. AIs were sent to destroy mankind. A hundered years before, Vivy was the first autonomous AI. Her mission is to spread happiness and joy through her voice. She strives to pour her heart in her performances, until an AI from the future recruits her to stop the war which is a hundred year in the making.
