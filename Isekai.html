Isekai

That Time I Got Reincarnated As A Slime

This story revolves around Rimuru Tempest, once a human, but was reincarnated in a different world as a slime. In the cave where he was reincarnated, Rimuru met The Storm Dragon Veldora who named him as Rimuru Tempest and gave him abilities beyond his slime race. With his new body and abilities; Rimuru starts a new journey, a new life, a new story entitled "That Time I Got Reincarnated As A Slime".

The Rising of the Shield Hero

Naofumi Iwatani was summoned into another world and became one of the Four Cardinal Heroes. He was given the title "Shield Hero". Alongside the Spear, Bow and Sword hero, Naofumi must protect Melromarc from a plague of monsters known as the Waves of Catastrophe. After being accused of crime, he must find away to fight the waves even if he only has a shield.

Mushoku Tensei

Mushoku Tensei is an anime about adventure. The anime's season 1 part 2 is currently airing (Nov. 2021). The story is about a thirty-fouryear-old man who attempted something heroic, but ended up in an accident. He was reincarnated in another world where he awakens as a baby named Rudeus Greyrat. With his memories of his past life, he easily adpated to this new world. As time passes, he was able to learn the new language, sowrdsmanship and magic. This where his new journey begins.

Sekai Saiko no Ansatsusha

The anime is the story Lugh. He was reincarnated by a goddess who seeks strong people to beome heroes. As a trained assassin in his previous life, Lugh excelled in his abilities with his mission to defeat criminals. He recruited multiple member to help him in his job, to put people in their place in this new world.
